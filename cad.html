How to connect to our CAD

Users can create an account, and enter in the community ID in the "Join Community" popup (7PGVAL8).

Users can search using the Community ID and press the "Join" button to add your community. Once joined you should see a "Permission Key" section type "civilianacccess" into that section to be Granted the Civilian Section of the CAD.

From there, users can select the community card in the "My Communites" section to login to the CAD.

How to create a Civilian

Firstly, click on the Civilian Tab on your CAD, from there you should notice some buttons at the top of your screen.
Press on the 'NEW CHARACTER' button. You'll now see a new box popup. Fill in the relivent text boxes below the Civlian Photo

e.g First, Last. MI = Middle Initial. DOB = Date of Birth.

If you fail to fill this out correctly the DMV and Law Enforcement Officers can revoke your license and deny your access to getting a license.

How to add a License

Firstly, click on the DMV Button and then select License. Here you'll be able to add the following Licenses; Driving, Weapon and Hunting
When filling our your license you need to make sure you fill it our correctly. If your information is wrong on your Civilian Profile it'll be wrong on here. You've got the option to set your license as: Pending, Valid, Revoked or Suspended.

Make sure to set the right Expiration date or you'll either have your license revoked or get arrested. TIP: Set it for a Year in the Future.

How to register a Vehicle

Firstly, click on the DMV Button and then select VEHICLE REGISTRATION. Here you'll be able to add all the details to do with your vehicle. You'll also be able to set your vehicle as: Pending, Valid, Expired, Suspended or Stolen. When filling out your registration make sure it is all correct or you can get arrested for not having valid registration.

TIP: With the Vehicle Year have a look at their real-life counterpart and see what their date of manufacture is.
TIP #2: With the Expiration do the same as your license, set it to 1 Year in the Future.
